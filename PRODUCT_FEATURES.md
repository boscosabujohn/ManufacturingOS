# ManufacturingOS - Product Features

## Overview
ManufacturingOS is a comprehensive ERP solution designed specifically for manufacturing companies to streamline operations from sales to delivery.

---

## 1. SALES & CRM

### Lead Management
- Capture and track leads from multiple sources
- Lead scoring and qualification
- Convert leads to customers with one click
- Track all customer interactions and communication history

### Quotation Management
- Create professional quotations with line items
- Version control for quote revisions
- Multi-level approval workflow for discounts
- Convert approved quotes to sales orders

### Sales Order Processing
- Create sales orders with multiple line items
- Attach customer PO, drawings, and specifications
- Automatic order confirmation and acknowledgment
- Real-time order status tracking

### Configure-Price-Quote (CPQ)
- Interactive product configurator
- Dynamic pricing based on configuration
- Guided selling for complex products
- Automated quote generation

---

## 2. PRODUCTION PLANNING

### Bill of Materials (BOM)
- Multi-level BOM with parent-child relationships
- BOM versioning and revision history
- Automatic material requirement calculation
- Cost rollup from components to finished goods

### Work Order Management
- Create work orders from sales orders
- Schedule production with target dates
- Track work order progress through stages
- View production status on Gantt charts

### Shop Floor Operations
- Real-time production tracking
- Support for multi-stage manufacturing (Cutting → Bending → Welding → Painting → Assembly → QC → Packing)
- Machine and equipment logging
- Production quantity entry and tracking

### Work Center Management
- Define production work centers with capacity
- Standard operation times and costs
- Capacity utilization monitoring
- Labor and overhead cost tracking

---

## 3. PROCUREMENT

### Purchase Requisitions
- Auto-generate from production requirements
- Manual requisition creation
- Multi-level approval based on value
- Convert approved PRs to purchase orders

### Purchase Order Management
- Create POs from requisitions or directly
- Compare vendor quotes side-by-side
- PO approval workflow
- Track PO status from draft to closure

### Goods Receipt
- Receive materials against POs
- Quality inspection at receipt
- Three-way matching (PO-GRN-Invoice)
- Handle partial receipts and returns

### Vendor Management
- Maintain vendor master with contacts
- Track vendor performance and ratings
- Manage payment terms and methods
- Vendor compliance documentation

---

## 4. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

### Stock Control
- Real-time inventory visibility
- Multi-warehouse management
- Batch and serial number tracking
- Multiple valuation methods (FIFO, LIFO, Weighted Average)

### Stock Movements
- Inter-warehouse transfers
- Stock adjustments with reason codes
- Automatic stock updates from production
- Full transaction history

### Warehouse Operations
- Bin-level location management
- Receiving and putaway
- Picking and dispatch
- Cycle counting and physical inventory

### Reorder Management
- Set reorder points and quantities
- Low stock alerts
- Automatic purchase requisition generation
- ABC analysis for inventory optimization

---

## 5. QUALITY MANAGEMENT

### Inspection Management
- Define quality parameters and tolerances
- Schedule inspections at key checkpoints
- Record inspection results
- Pass/Fail determination with evidence

### Non-Conformance Reports (NCR)
- Log quality issues and defects
- Classify by defect type and severity
- Root cause analysis
- Track resolution status

### Corrective & Preventive Actions (CAPA)
- Define corrective actions for NCRs
- Assign responsibility and deadlines
- Track implementation progress
- Verify effectiveness before closure

### Quality Dashboard
- Real-time quality metrics
- Defect trend analysis
- First pass yield tracking
- Supplier quality performance
